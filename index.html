Pharmacy Consultation Training

Practice your consultation skills with an AI-powered patient simulator using D-ID Streams

📋 Instructions

  1. Enter your n8n webhook URLs in the Configuration section
  2. Load your Google Sheet with patient scenarios
  3. Select a patient scenario from the dropdown (or choose random)
  1. Initialize your D-ID Streams avatar with your API key
  2. Start the stream connection to enable real-time conversation
  3. Hold down the "Push to Talk" button and speak your consultation
  1. The AI patient will respond in real-time through the avatar
  2. Continue the conversation naturally
  3. Use "Submit Chat" to get evaluation feedback

Tips: The Streams API provides real-time interaction - speak naturally and the avatar will respond immediately!
